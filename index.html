Customer Journey

Lifecycle from application to bank credit

Live Flow

Registration & Application

User registers with mobile OTP and fills basic details (Name, Income, Employment) on Bharatloan app/web.

Digital KYC Flow

System performs real-time PAN validation, Aadhaar e-KYC, and selfie-based liveness detection for identity proof.

Underwriting & Approval

Automated credit scoring using bureau data and bank statement analysis. Instant loan offer generation based on risk profile.

Digital Agreement (e-Sign)

User reviews loan terms and signs the agreement via Aadhaar OTP. Repayment mandate (e-NACH) is registered.

Final Loan Disbursal

System triggers automated IMPS/NEFT. Funds are credited to the user's bank account within 15-30 minutes.
