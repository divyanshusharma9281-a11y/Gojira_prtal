BharatLoan Customer Journey: Step-by-Step SOP

This document outlines the end-to-end customer journey for BharatLoan as defined in the Gojira TP training portal. It pairs customer actions with the necessary backend Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).

Step 1: Registration

Customer Activity:

The customer visits the portal and enters their mobile number. They receive and enter an OTP (One-Time Password) to verify their identity.

Backend SOP:

  • System triggers an automated OTP via the gateway.
  • Validation of user identity and active session management.
  • Capture of initial lead metadata (device info, timestamp, referral source).

Step 2: KYC Submission

Customer Activity:

The customer uploads their National ID (Aadhaar/PAN) and performs a live selfie capture for biometric matching.

Backend SOP:

  • Automated OCR extracts data from the ID.
  • Liveness check to prevent spoofing or fraud.
  • Cross-referencing with government databases (UIDAI / IT Department).

Step 3: Financial Verification

Customer Activity:

The customer connects their primary bank account through an Account Aggregator or uploads digital bank statements.

Backend SOP:

  • Automated analysis of last 6 months of transactions.
  • Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio calculation.
  • Triggering the internal risk scoring engine.

Step 4: Loan Selection

Customer Activity:

Based on the approved limit, the customer selects the loan amount and repayment tenure.

Backend SOP:

  • Real-time EMI generation.
  • Validation against maximum risk threshold.
  • Generation of digital Sanction Letter.

Step 5: E-Sign Agreement

Customer Activity:

The customer reviews and electronically signs the digital loan agreement.

Backend SOP:

  • Generation of legally binding digital contract.
  • Secure storage with timestamping.
  • Triggering e-Mandate (NACH) setup.

Step 6: Final Verification

Customer Activity:

Customer may receive a verification call or confirm bank details.

Backend SOP:

  • Human-in-the-loop or automated compliance audit.
  • Bank account ownership verification (Penny-drop).
  • AML and blacklist checks.

Step 7: Disbursal

Customer Activity:

Customer receives notification and loan amount is credited.

Backend SOP:

  • IMPS/NEFT payment trigger.
  • Loan status updated to Active in CBS.
  • Automated SMS/Email with repayment schedule.

Key Operational Notes

  • Most steps are automated to ensure instant approval.
  • Support teams may assist if customers stall at Step 3 or 5.
  • Timely repayment is critical to maintain a healthy CIBIL score.
