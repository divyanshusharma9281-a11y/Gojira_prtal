Compliance Knowledge Base

What is an NBFC?
Financial institution providing loans/insurance but not engaging in agriculture or industrial operations. Registered under Companies Act 1956; cannot accept public deposits.
What is the difference between a Bank and an NBFC?

• NBFCs cannot accept demand deposits.

• NBFCs cannot issue cheques drawn on themselves.

• Deposit insurance facility of DICGC is not available to depositors of NBFCs.

What is a Personal Loan?
An unsecured loan provided by a lender for personal expenses (medical, wedding, travel). It does not require collateral and is granted based on the borrower's creditworthiness.
What is Account Aggregator (AA)?
Script: "It is an RBI-approved safe way where you allow us to check your bank details online. This makes loan processing faster."
  • • No money will be deducted from your account.
  • • Fully controlled by you; you can stop access anytime.
  • • Safe and encrypted data transfer.
Can I repay early?
Yes, you can repay prior to the due date. This helps in maintaining a healthy credit score and avoids the accumulation of additional daily interest.
