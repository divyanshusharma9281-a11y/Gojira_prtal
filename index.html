01

Customer Registration

Customer registers on BharatLoan platform using mobile number. OTP verification is mandatory to activate the account.

02

Basic Profile Creation

Customer fills basic details such as full name, date of birth, address, and employment type.

03

KYC Verification

PAN and Aadhaar details are collected and verified via internal systems or DigiLocker for authenticity.

04

Loan Application Submission

Customer selects loan amount and tenure, and submits income and employment-related information.

05

Credit Bureau Check

System fetches customer credit score and repayment history from authorized credit bureaus.

06

Risk & Eligibility Assessment

Customer eligibility is evaluated based on credit score, income, liabilities, and internal risk rules.

07

Loan Offer Generation

Approved customers receive a loan offer with sanctioned amount, interest rate, EMI, and tenure.

08

Offer Acceptance & eMandate

Customer accepts the loan offer and completes eNACH / eMandate setup for EMI auto-debit.

09

Loan Agreement eSign

Customer digitally signs the loan agreement through Aadhaar-based or OTP-based eSign.

10

Loan Disbursal
